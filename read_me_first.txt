CESKY
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Tento repozitar obsahuje prepisy koverzaci s LLMs. Ve slozkach jsou take CSV soubory, jejicz prepis LLMs na vstupu dostaly zadany. V nekterych slozkach je k dispozici i CSV, vystup pro EasyMiner
kody v jazyce Python. Cislovani je shodne s cisly experimentu v BP. V kazde cislovane slozce jsou podslozky venujici se kazdemu modelu zvlast.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
U data publikaci jednotlivych prepisu konverzaci se ridte primarne udaji uvedenymi v plnem textu bakalarske prace, nebot po prejmenovani chatu se datum u chatu uvedene v LLMs mohlo pozmenit.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ENGLISH
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This repository contains transcripts of conversations with LLMs. In some directories, CSV files provided in the prompt, output from EasyMiner and Python code are also available. The enumeration
corresponds to the number of experiments in the thesis. Each enumerated directory consists of subdirectories concerning each model separately.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
By the dates of the transcriptions of the conversations, please primarily follow the dates given v full text of the thesis, because after renaming the chat, the date mentioned in LLM's chat could
change.
